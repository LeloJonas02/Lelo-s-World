Her Style Inspiration

Lelo's style is inspired by many era's but her favourites are from the 2014 era, also known as the "swag" era.

She loves to wear oversized clothes, graphic tees, and sneakers. She also loves to accessorize with hats, sunglasses, and jewelry. Lelo's style is all about being comfortable and confident in her own skin.

She often mixes and matches different styles to create her own unique look. Lelo is not afraid to take risks with her fashion choices, and she loves to experiment with new trends.

Some of her favourite fashion icons include Rihanna, Zendaya, and Uncle Waffles. She admires their confidence and individuality, and she often draws inspiration from their outfits.
